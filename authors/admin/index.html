Ahmad A. Rushdi

Research Scientist

Sandia National Laboratories

I am a Member of Technical Staff at the Center for Computing Research of Sandia National Laboratories, where I work on computational and data science research problems, as part of the Dakota team. I’m virtually located in the Bay Area, CA.

I have previously held R&D and management positions at Northrop Grumman Corporation in Albuquerque NM, the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of UC Davis in Davis CA, the Computational Visualization Center under Oden Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences at UT Austin in Austin TX, and Cisco Systems in San Jose CA.

Thanks for visiting my webpage. Please feel free to reach out if you have any questions, and stay safe!
