#About

I'm a research scientist and Director of Industry Programs at Stanford HAI, where I lead research collaborations and executive education initiatives translating AI, ML, and foundation-model research into applied real-world solutions. My research spans uncertainty quantification (UQ) for scientific machine learning (SciML) and broader work on AI trust, safety, privacy, and responsible development.

Previously: Sandia National Laboratories (Dakota), Northrop Grumman, research fellow at UC Davis and the Oden Institute at UT Austin, R&D engineer at Cisco. Ph.D., ECE, UC Davis. M.S./B.S., Cairo University.
