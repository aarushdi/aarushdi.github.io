About

I am a research scientist and Director of Industry Programs at Stanford HAI, where I lead research collaborations and executive education initiatives that translate AI, ML, and foundation-model research into applied real-world solutions. My research interests span uncertainty quantification (UQ) for scientific machine learning (SciML) and broader areas of AI trust, safety, privacy, and responsible development.

Previously: research scientist at Sandia National Laboratories (Dakota team), R&D Manager of Data Science at Northrop Grumman, research fellow at UC Davis and the Oden Institute at UT Austin, and R&D engineer at Cisco. Ph.D. in ECE (minor in Applied Math) from UC Davis; M.S./B.S. from Cairo University. Senior Member of IEEE; member of ACM and SIAM.
