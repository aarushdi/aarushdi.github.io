About

I am a research scientist and Director of Industry Programs at Stanford HAI, where I lead research collaborations and executive education initiatives translating AI, ML, and foundation-model research into applied real-world solutions. My research spans uncertainty quantification for scientific machine learning and broader work on AI trust, safety, and responsible development.

Previously at Sandia National Laboratories, Northrop Grumman, UC Davis, the Oden Institute at UT Austin, and Cisco. Ph.D. in ECE from UC Davis; M.S./B.S. from Cairo University.
