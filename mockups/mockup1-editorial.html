About

I am a research scientist and Director of Industry Programs at Stanford HAI, where I lead research collaborations and executive education initiatives that translate AI, ML, and foundation-model research into applied real-world solutions. My research spans uncertainty quantification for scientific machine learning and broader work on AI trust, safety, and responsible development.

Previously: research scientist at Sandia National Laboratories (Dakota team); R&D Manager of Data Science at Northrop Grumman; research fellow at UC Davis and the Oden Institute at UT Austin; R&D engineer at Cisco. Ph.D. in ECE from UC Davis; M.S./B.S. from Cairo University.
